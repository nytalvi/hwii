Detransitioning

Written by polly@hwii.net on 7/25/2025

Trigger warning: Speaking about self-harm, eating disorders, suicidal ideation, gender dysphoria, etc. Please be careful, and do not read this post if you're not in the right headspace for it. Thank you, and I'll see you in the next one.

As I watch from my window the world pass by, I look down and realize I'm right where I need to be.

Effective immediately, I am a man whose name is Ezra.

Snow continues to fall upon my broken heart as I reach for my Rosary. God watches over my soul wehn I sleep, read, cry, and pray.

No longer a girl, I will still continue to serve and love my friends and the queer community, and everyone else in the world.
