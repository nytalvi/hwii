Hello, everyone! I return once again as Ezra[1], and this is my whimsical and wondrous website. My favorite things are religion, writing, programming, and video games.

I'm a Roman Catholic, and I very deeply value the teachings of Christ. I believe in equal rights for all people, poverty, chastity, obedience, humility, and the most important commandment of all: love.

It should also be noted that, while I enjoy attending a Latin Mass here and there, and I do adore the 1962 Roman missal, I do not consider myself to be a traditional Catholic, or "tradcat," nor "radtrad" either. I am just a Catholic, because that's what matters, not politics. Do not interact with me if you're going to deny Vatican II or climate change, just read the Bible.
