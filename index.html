Hello, everyone! I return once again as Basil, and this is my whimsical and wondrous website. My favorite things are religion, writing, programming, and video games.

I'm a Roman Catholic, and I very deeply value the teachings of Christ. I believe in equal rights for all people, poverty, chastity, obedience, humility, and the most important commandment of all: love.

I also develop little projects and programs for fun. My language of choice is usually Rust, though I've been learning C99 (thanks to some lainons).

Such projects can be found on my GitHub, linked below.

I've once again resurrected my website, since I want to get back into writing and programming again after a very long break. In the time that I've spent off the screen, I've changed quite a bit. My interests have shifted, my behavior has evolved, and things seem to make a bit more sense now.

Obviously, I'm still working hard on my website and on myself, so bear with me as I figure all of this stuff out. Below you can find a list of my writings. Some people call them blog posts, but I feel like that's too fancy for them. They're writings, just like what you'd find on the back of a cereal box, but even less comprehensible.
