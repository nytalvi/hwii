Hello, everyone! I return once again as Ezra[1], and this is my whimsical and wondrous website. My favorite things are religion, writing, programming, and video games.

I'm a Roman Catholic, and I very deeply value the teachings of Christ. I believe in equal rights for all people, poverty, chastity, obedience, humility, and the most important commandment of all: love.

I've once again resurrected my website, since I want to get back into writing and programming again after a very long break. In the time that I've spent off the screen, I've changed quite a bit. My interests have shifted, my behavior has evolved, and things seem to make a bit more sense now.

Obviously, I'm still working hard on my website and on myself, so bear with me as I figure all of this stuff out. Below you can find a list of my writings.
